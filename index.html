Proposed Approach
🧭 Guiding Principle

The proposed system will augment existing processes without replacing or disrupting them. It operates as a non-intrusive overlay with minimal workflow changes and measurable outcomes within a short duration.

🧈 Phase 1 Pilot: Ghee Supply Chain Tracking (90 Days)

Selected for its high procurement value, critical importance in laddu preparation, quality sensitivity, and frequent audit relevance.

A
Procurement & Vendor Layer
  • Digital onboarding of selected vendors
  • Batch-level tagging of supplied materials
  • Vendor performance capture
B
Inward & Storage
  • Digital recording of inward receipts
  • Warehouse-level inventory visibility
  • Batch identification and tracking
C
Consumption Tracking
  • Batch-wise consumption at kitchen level
  • Mapping input materials to production output
D
Dashboard & Analytics
  • Real-time stock visibility
  • Batch traceability reports
  • Consumption vs procurement reconciliation
  • Anomaly alerts and deviations
