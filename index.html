01 Overview

What Farview Is

Farview is building an AI-powered operating system for complex, multi-year projects, starting with industries like shipbuilding and real estate development.

It acts as a decision layer above existing systems — Procore, Primavera P6, Yardi, CRM — bringing together fragmented data to identify risks earlier and improve how teams make decisions.

The platform is built on data and AI systems previously deployed across large-scale environments, including work with UNICEF, Gavi, Pfizer, and government programs.

02 The Problem

Critical data trapped in disconnected systems

Across development and construction, critical project data exists across multiple disconnected systems. Even with strong teams and tools, organizations typically:

Late Risk Identification

Cost and schedule risks are typically identified weeks after they form, leaving little time to course-correct.

Manual Coordination

Heavy reliance on manual coordination and reporting creates bottlenecks and increases error risk at every handoff.

No Unified View

Lack of a unified view across teams and systems means no single source of truth for project health or decision-making.

"This is not a data problem — it is a decision visibility problem."

05 Pilot Overview — Phase 0

A Structured Validation

A structured validation of the Farview platform. Objective: test whether Farview can improve early visibility into risk and decision-making within a defined workflow.

Scope

  • 1 team — Marketing / Sales
  • 1–2 specific workflows
  • Read-only access to existing systems
  • Onsite deployment — Palm Beach County

Approach

  • Short diagnostic to identify breakdowns in visibility
  • Configure Farview's platform to selected workflows
  • Deploy a working prototype focused on real decision points

Deliverables

Working Farview prototype
Weekly insights and observations
Final evaluation of impact and next steps
Note

This is a platform deployment and configuration, not a custom build.

07 Decision Gate

Evidence-Driven Continuation

At the midpoint and end of the pilot, the decision to continue is governed by clear evidence of value — not momentum or commitment.

"Continue only if there is clear evidence that Farview improves decision visibility or timing."

If no signal is found, the pilot is stopped.

Signal Found

Proceed to controlled expansion — extend to additional workflows, introduce structured dashboards, validate repeatability across teams.

No Signal

Pilot is stopped. No further obligation or commitment. Clean exit with full evaluation report provided.
