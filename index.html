Company Overview

StaTwig Technology Services is a technology company focused on digitizing and optimizing supply chains at scale, with proven expertise across public health, logistics, and government ecosystems. Established in 2016 and headquartered in Hyderabad, StaTwig builds secure, scalable, and data-driven platforms using blockchain, AI/ML, IoT, and real-time analytics.


Its solutions enable end-to-end visibility, traceability, and operational control — helping organizations improve efficiency, reduce leakages, and strengthen last-mile delivery. Trusted by global institutions, StaTwig delivers national-scale implementations that support critical programs with real-time insights and decision intelligence.
