The Problem

Data exists. Visibility doesn't.

Across development and construction, critical project data lives across multiple disconnected systems. Schedule lives in Primavera P6. Costs sit in Procore or Yardi. Consultant reports arrive as PDFs. Operational updates come via email.

Even with strong teams and modern tools, cost and schedule risks are consistently identified weeks — sometimes months — after they have already formed. By the time a risk appears in a monthly report, the window for low-cost intervention has often closed.

The challenge is not a lack of data. It is a lack of early, cross-system visibility.

"The risk wasn't invisible — it was just in a system no one had time to cross-reference."
